A brief summary of Paul Kim's life so far

My name is Paul Kim. I've been told that my story is very unusual; to save you some time, I've condensed it to the following! Born to Canadian-Korean parents in Seoul, Korea, I was schooled in English at Seoul Foreign School. In 2002, we emigrated back to Canada and I spent 2 years in Quebec where I learned a bit of French. In 2004, I moved to Vancouver for the rest of secondary school and entered UBC in 2008. Graduating far too late (2014), I briefly worked as a technical writer and then worked for an IT company in the UK in 2018. I returned and found employment difficult, so I decided to bite the bullet and properly learn IT!

Once you get used to it, you start seeing HTML everywhere!
